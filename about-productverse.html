Frequently Asked Questions

Why are you sending this newsletter? 

For a decade I've been reading 100+ articles a week.

I first started this newsletter to coach my team but during the first lockdown, I decided to make a public version.


I firmly believe that being part of a community is the best way to learn.

What is in for me?

I now bring the best content to your inbox. You'll find interesting reads, personal growth tips & useful tools to improve your craft.

Here a few examples of the most popular content shared yet:

  • The top 10 deliverables of Product Managers by Sachin Rekhi
  • Learning Product Design as a Beginner (52 Free Resources) by Caitlin Sowers
  • Why “Standups” are Useless and How to Run Great Product Team Meetings by Andy Johns
  • The Mental Cost of Being a Product Manager by Lilly M. Dobreva

My manifesto:

  • Short: I always choose quality over quantity
  • Relevant, not new: I've been working & collecting resources on products for 10y+. I'll share with you the most insightful content, from 20 years ago or yesterday. Timeless advice.
  • Humans, not algorithms: everything I share, I've read it, and is of some help for my day to day job.
